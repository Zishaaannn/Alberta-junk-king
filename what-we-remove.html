What We Cannot Remove

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot remove:

  • Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, asbestos)
  • Medical waste or biohazard materials
  • Explosives or ammunition
  • Radioactive materials
  • Live animals or deceased pets
  • Food waste or perishables

Need Help with Restricted Items?

If you have items we cannot remove, we can provide recommendations for proper disposal methods and specialized services in your area.
