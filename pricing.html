Pricing FAQ

How do you calculate pricing?

Our pricing is based on the volume of items and the space they take up in our truck. We provide free on-site estimates to give you the most accurate pricing.

Are there any hidden fees?

No! Our pricing is 100% upfront and all-inclusive. The price we quote includes pickup, transportation, and proper disposal. No hidden fees or surprise charges.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! We offer same-day and next-day service throughout Alberta. Contact us early in the day for the best availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the job.
